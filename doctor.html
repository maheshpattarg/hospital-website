Your consulting doctor is Dr. Sagar Sangamesh Kamble, an ayurvedic practitioner dedicated to guiding health through nature. Dr. Kamble has completed his UG degree from KLE University, Shri BMK Ayurveda Mahavidyalaya, Belagavi, and pursued his MD from Sri Dharmasthala Manjunatheshwar Ayurveda Mahavidyalaya, Udupi. He is currently pursuing a PhD from SDM College of Ayurveda, Udupi.

Currently, he serves as the Chief Consultant at Ayushman Health Care, Belagavi, and also works as an Assistant Professor at SBG Ayurveda Medical College, Belagavi, as well as a Consultant at SBG Ayurveda Hospital, Belagavi.
